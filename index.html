Common Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Synergent.

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects go live in 2-4 weeks. Simple automations can be deployed within days. Complex multi-system integrations might take 6-8 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline during discovery.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?

No. We speak business, not just tech. Harry (your manager) translates between your goals and our technical execution. You focus on outcomes, we handle the implementation.

What happens after deployment?

We don't disappear after launch. All projects include 30 days of support, documentation, and training. Most clients move to a monthly retainer for ongoing optimization and new features.

How do you handle data security?

We're paranoid about security. All systems follow least-privilege access, data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we never train models on your proprietary data. We can sign NDAs and work within your compliance requirements.

Can you integrate with our existing tools?

Absolutely. We specialize in connecting disparate systems. Whether you're on Salesforce, HubSpot, Notion, Slack, or custom internal tools, we build bridges that make everything work together.

What if the AI makes a mistake?

Humans review critical decisions, especially in the beginning. We build confidence thresholds and escalation paths. Over time, the system gets better and requires less oversight ‚Äî but the human layer never disappears.
